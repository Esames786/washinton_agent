Hello Transport — Quote System Explained

Complete guide: How quotes work, roles, filtering, and dispatcher assignment

1. The Two Primary Roles

🧑‍💼 Order Taker (OT)

What they do: They are the SALES people. They call customers, create new quotes, follow up, and book orders.

Their job ends at: BOOKED (pstatus=8) — once an order is booked, their main job is done.

Key field on order: order_taker_id = their user ID

Screens they use: NEW → INTERESTED → FOLLOW UP → ASKING LOW → BOOKED

🚛 Dispatcher

What they do: They are the LOGISTICS people. They take booked orders and arrange carriers, schedule pickups, track deliveries.

Their job starts at: LISTED (pstatus=9) — after OT books and lists the order.

Key field on order: dispatcher_id = their user ID (assigned manually)

Screens they use: LISTED → DISPATCH → PICKED UP → DELIVERED → COMPLETED

4. All Quotes vs Own Quotes vs Group Quotes

This setting is on each user's profile. It controls what orders they can SEE in their sidebar screens.

SettingValueOrder Taker sees...Dispatcher sees...
All Quotes order_taker_quote = 0 ALL orders in the system (no filter) ALL orders in the system (no filter)
Own Quotes order_taker_quote = 1 Only orders where order_taker_id = their ID Only orders where dispatcher_id = their ID
Group Quotes order_taker_quote = 2 Orders in their manager's group (FIND_IN_SET on manager_ot_ids) NOT APPLICABLE for Dispatcher
⚠️ Key difference: For OT, "Own" checks order_taker_id. For Dispatcher, "Own" checks dispatcher_id. These are DIFFERENT fields on the order. A dispatcher won't see an order under "Own" until someone assigns them as the dispatcher.

6. What is price_request_assign_dispatcher?

❓ Your Question: Is this the individual quote assignment screen?

Answer: NO. This is a DIFFERENT, SEPARATE feature. It is NOT the same as assigning a dispatcher to an individual order.


price_request_assign_dispatcher

Route: GET /price_request_assign_dispatcher

What it does: Shows a list of ALL available dispatchers so that a dispatcher can be given access to the "Price Check" feature.

Purpose: It's about giving a dispatcher PERMISSION to see price check requests — not about assigning them to a specific order.

Think of it as: "Which dispatchers are allowed to work on price requests?"

assign_to_dispatcher (Individual Order)

Route: POST /assign_to_dispatcher

What it does: Assigns a specific dispatcher to a specific order by setting dispatcher_id on that order.

Purpose: This IS the individual quote assignment. "Order #123 is now assigned to Dispatcher John."

Think of it as: "Who is handling this specific shipment?"

9. Summary — What You Need to Know

✅ Order Taker creates quotes → order_taker_id is set to their ID

✅ Dispatcher is assigned to orders AFTER booking → dispatcher_id is set manually

✅ Own Quotes for OT = orders they created. Own Quotes for Dispatcher = orders assigned to them

✅ All Quotes = no filter, see everything

✅ Group Quotes = only for OT/CSR/Seller Agent, NOT for Dispatcher

✅ price_request_assign_dispatcher = NOT individual order assignment. It is about price check permissions for dispatchers. Ignore it for quote assignment.

10. What is Private OT?

🔒 Private OT — A Special Type of Order Taker

Private OT is a flag (private_OT = 1) that can be set on any Order Taker user. It separates them from the regular OT pool for revenue/commission reporting purposes.


Regular Order Taker (private_OT = 0)

Appears in the standard Employee Revenue report (/view_employee_revenue)

Commission is calculated using the standard CommissionRange table (based on performance brackets)

Visible to managers in the regular OT revenue screen

Private OT (private_OT = 1)

Appears in a separate Private OT Revenue report (/view_employee_revenue_PrivateOT)

Commission is taken from their own UserCommission.commission field directly — NOT from the range table

Hidden from the regular OT revenue screen — kept private/separate

Revenue Report Screen/view_employee_revenue/view_employee_revenue_PrivateOT

Visible in regular OT list✓ Yes✗ No (hidden)

DB columnprivate_OT = 0private_OT = 1
⚠️ Important: Private OT is ONLY about revenue/commission reporting separation. It does NOT change how the OT creates quotes, sees quotes, or works in the system. The only difference is which revenue report they appear in and how their commission is calculated.
💡 Use case: If you have a special agent with a custom fixed commission deal (not based on performance brackets), you mark them as Private OT. Their commission is tracked separately and not mixed with the regular OT performance reports.
